My involvment with music

If you've read the bio on my main page you already know that I like spending my free time listening to or playing music. From jamming along to tracks on my pretty old Washburn guitar or just straight up improvising, I try to express myself as much as I possibly can. When I'm not playing music I'm most likely listening to it, from post-punk and darkwave all the way to japanese fusion jazz and sludge there's always something new for me to discover. Alongside the albums themselves I like the artwork presented with them as it can help the listener better understand the concept the artist had in mind and their feelings at the time.
